📄 Pakistan's Constitutional Conundrum

Navigating the Lucid Implications of the 26th Amendment

Published in the Pakistan Research Journal of Social Sciences (PRJSS), this publication critically examines the constitutional, institutional, and jurisprudential implications of the Twenty-Sixth Constitutional Amendment and its impact upon Pakistan's democratic and legal architecture.

The article evaluates questions of judicial independence, constitutional restructuring, separation of powers, and institutional transformation through a research-driven analytical framework.
