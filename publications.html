📚 Pakistan's Constitutional Conundrum

Navigating the Lucid Implications of the 26th Amendment

Published in the Pakistan Research Journal of Social Sciences (PRJSS) in August 2025, this article critically examines the constitutional, institutional, and jurisprudential implications of the Twenty-Sixth Constitutional Amendment and its potential impact on Pakistan's evolving legal and democratic framework.

The article evaluates questions of judicial independence, constitutional restructuring, separation of powers, and the long-term consequences of institutional transformation through a research-driven and analytical perspective.
